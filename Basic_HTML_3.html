Tenjho Tenge Characters

Souichiro Nagi

The main protagonist in the manga

Bob Makihara

Powerful companion of Souichiro Nagi.

Maya Natsume

One of the main protaganists in the manga and anime series. She is the younger sibling of the late Shin Natsume and the elder sister of Aya Natsume.

Aya Natsume

Younger sibling to Maya Natsume and the late Shin Natsume.

Masataka Takayanagi

Your average high school student at first glance, but he is a surprisingly powerful martial artist. He is always a formidable fighter, but he becomes much more dangerous when he is serious or angered.
